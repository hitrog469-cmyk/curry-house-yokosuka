# Gallery Photos Setup

## Instructions for Adding Photos

This folder is for restaurant gallery photos that will be displayed on the Gallery page.

### Photo Requirements:
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WEBP
- **Size**: Recommended 800x800px (square format works best)
- **Quality**: High quality photos showing your dishes, restaurant interior, or happy customers
- **File Size**: Keep under 2MB per image for fast loading

### Naming Convention:
Please rename your photos using this format:
- `gallery-001.jpg`
- `gallery-002.jpg`
- `gallery-003.jpg`
- etc.

### Example Photos to Include:
1. **Dishes**: Beautiful food photography of your signature curries
2. **Restaurant**: Interior shots showing ambiance and dining area
3. **Customers**: Happy customers enjoying meals (with permission)
4. **Chef/Staff**: Team members preparing food
5. **Special Events**: Catering events, parties, celebrations

### Steps to Add Photos:
1. Copy your photos into this folder (`public/images/gallery/`)
2. Rename them following the naming convention above
3. The website will automatically display them in the gallery
